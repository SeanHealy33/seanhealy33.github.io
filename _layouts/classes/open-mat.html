Enhance Your Skills in a Free-Form Environment

At Athlete A, our Open Mat sessions provide a unique opportunity for practitioners of all levels to refine their Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu skills in a relaxed, unstructured setting. These sessions are perfect for those looking to drill techniques, spar with different partners, or work on specific aspects of their game.

Our experienced instructors are on hand to provide guidance and answer questions, but the focus is on self-directed learning and exploration. Open Mat is an excellent complement to structured classes, allowing you to apply what you've learned and discover new aspects of your BJJ journey.

Benefits of Open Mat Training

Personalized Practice

Focus on the techniques and positions you want to improve, tailoring your training to your specific needs.

Diverse Training Partners

Roll with a variety of partners, exposing yourself to different styles and body types to enhance your adaptability.

Increased Mat Time

Get extra training time to supplement your regular classes and accelerate your progress.

Problem Solving

Work through challenging positions and scenarios at your own pace, developing creative solutions.

Community Building

Connect with fellow BJJ enthusiasts, share knowledge, and build lasting friendships on and off the mats.

Competition Preparation

Simulate competition-style rolls and prepare mentally and physically for tournaments.
