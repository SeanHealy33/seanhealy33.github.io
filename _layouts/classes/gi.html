Master the Art of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

At Athlete A, our Gi BJJ program is designed to help practitioners of all levels develop their skills in the traditional form of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. Our classes focus on techniques that utilize the gi, emphasizing grip fighting, submissions, and positional control unique to gi training.

Our experienced instructors create a challenging yet supportive environment where students can learn, grow, and push their limits. Through Gi BJJ, practitioners develop technical proficiency, physical conditioning, and mental resilience that benefit them both on and off the mats.

Benefits of Gi BJJ Training

Technical Precision

Gi BJJ emphasizes precise technique and leverage, allowing practitioners to control and submit larger opponents.

Grip Strength

Training with the gi develops exceptional grip strength, benefiting both grappling and everyday activities.

Strategic Thinking

Gi BJJ is often compared to human chess, requiring practitioners to think several moves ahead.

Self-Defense

Gi techniques are highly applicable to real-world self-defense scenarios where opponents are likely to be clothed.

Physical Conditioning

The added resistance of the gi provides an intense full-body workout, improving strength and endurance.

Mental Toughness

Overcoming challenges in Gi BJJ builds mental resilience that translates to all areas of life.
